Blackbird is an AI orchestration platform built around a simple principle: you should be able to use the AI models that best fit your business, security requirements, governance policies, and budget—not the models a platform vendor chooses for you.

## Bring Your Own AI
Blackbird's Bring Your Own AI approach gives organizations complete freedom to connect, orchestrate, and govern AI services from virtually any provider.

Whether you are using commercial foundation models, enterprise AI platforms, AI aggregators, open-source models, or proprietary models hosted in private environments, Blackbird acts as the orchestration layer that connects everything into automated business processes.

## Connect AI Your Way
Organizations can connect AI services using the authentication and deployment model that best fits their infrastructure and governance requirements.

Supported approaches include:
* API keys
* OAuth authentication
* Azure-native deployments
* Azure AI Foundry
* AWS Bedrock
* Enterprise AI gateways
* AI aggregation platforms such as Intento and Phrase Language AI
* Custom API endpoints
* Self-hosted AI services
* Private and fine-tuned models
* Models deployed behind corporate firewalls

This flexibility allows enterprises to maintain full control over security, compliance, procurement, and data residency requirements.

## Full Control Over Prompts and Model Behavior
Blackbird does not hide AI behind a black box.

Users maintain complete control over:
* System prompts
* User prompts
* Dynamic prompt generation
* Context injection
* Variables and workflow data
* Model parameters
* Temperature and creativity settings
* Vendor-specific options and controls

This enables organizations to create transparent, repeatable, and auditable AI-powered business processes tailored to their specific requirements.

## Built for Language and Content Workflows
Unlike generic AI platforms, Blackbird's AI applications are designed to understand structured language and content formats natively.

Supported formats include:
* XLIFF 2.2
* Microsoft Word (`.docx`)
* Microsoft PowerPoint (`.pptx`)
* Gettext (`.po`)
* XML
* JSON
* HTML
* And many other industry-standard content formats

By preserving document structure, metadata, segmentation, and localization context, Blackbird enables AI workflows that are significantly more reliable for translation, localization, content creation, review, and quality assurance use cases.

## No AI Markups or Hidden Fees
Blackbird does not resell AI consumption. There are no surcharges, markups, commissions, hidden fees, or token taxes added to your AI usage.

Organizations maintain direct commercial relationships with their AI providers and pay providers according to their own pricing models. This provides:
* Full pricing transparency
* Freedom from vendor lock-in
* Direct enterprise agreements with AI vendors
* Accurate cost allocation and chargeback
* Flexibility to switch providers at any time

Blackbird's role is to orchestrate AI-powered workflows—not to profit from AI consumption.

## Complete Observability Through Flights
Every process executed in Blackbird—called a **Flight**—creates a complete execution record.

Flights capture:
* Workflow inputs
* Workflow outputs
* Intermediate processing steps
* Prompt inputs
* AI responses
* Execution metadata
* Timestamps
* Performance metrics
* Model information
* Provider information

This creates a comprehensive audit trail for every AI-assisted business process.

### AI Usage Tracking and Cost Visibility
Where supported by the provider, Blackbird captures AI usage metadata, including:
* Token consumption
* Input token counts
* Output token counts
* Model versions
* Request identifiers
* Processing times
* Vendor-specific usage metrics

This enables organizations to monitor usage, allocate costs, optimize workflows, and measure AI ROI.

### Open Access to Operational Data
Flight metadata can be exported through Blackbird APIs and integrated into external systems such as:
* Data warehouses
* Business intelligence platforms
* Reporting tools
* Cost management systems
* Governance platforms
* Custom databases
* Analytics environments

Organizations retain full ownership and control of their operational and AI consumption data.

## Blacklake: Provenance and AI Governance
Flight records and associated metadata can also be stored in Blacklake, Blackbird's provenance and governance layer.

Blacklake enables organizations to maintain:
* End-to-end provenance
* Auditability
* Compliance records
* Operational governance
* AI governance
* Accountability
* Historical execution records

This provides a trusted system of record for both human-driven and AI-driven business processes.
